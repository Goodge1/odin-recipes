Taco

Description

The classic that once upon a time took the country by storm.

Traditional taco with a norwegian twist. Always a pleasure to invite friends for this social dish.

Ingredients

  • 4x Tortillas
  • 200g Meat
  • 1pc Taco spices
  • 1spoon Olive oil
  • 0.75dl Water
  • 1 Salad
  • 1 Tomato
  • 0.5 Corn
  • 1 Cucumber
  • Tacosauce
  • Cheese
  • Guacamole
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Heat meat in a pan with oil
  2. Add spicemix, water and let it sizzle on low heat for 10 minutes
  3. Add the tortillas in foil, and add to a pre-heated oven at 200 degrees for 2-3 minutes
  4. Serve with sauce, guacamole and a delicious salad & cheese
