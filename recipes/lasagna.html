Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish featuring layers of flat pasta alternated with fillings such as meat sauce, cheese, and vegetables.

It's seasoned with garlic and herbs, topped with melted cheese, and served in rich, flavorful squares.

Ingredients

  • Meat: 400g
  • Onion: 1
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • spices
  • Pasta plates
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers
