Pizza

Description

Pizza with tomatosauce, fresh mozarella & basil. Easy & increadibly delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pizzadough
  • 100g mozarella
  • 30g pizzasauce
  • 10g fresh basil
  • 1 spoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. Move pizzadough to room temprature in good time
  2. Let mozarella pour and then slice into pieces
  3. Bake out the dough. Squeese from the middle and outwards, so you get a nice crispy crust
  4. Spread pizzasauce, but not on the edges. Add mozarella
  5. Put the pizza in a preheated oven on 220 degrees.
  6. Remove pizza when a golden crispy color has formed. Top with olive oil and fresh basil.
